ZigCookie allows your site to meet its legal obligations in the European Union by alerting visitors that cookies are used.

== Description ==

**Due to abuse received from plugin repository users we are ceasing development of free WordPress plugins and this is the last release of this plugin. It will be removed from the repository in due course. Our pro-bono plugin development will now be exclusively for the ClassicPress platform.**

ZigCookie allows your site to meet its legal obligations in the European Union by alerting visitors that cookies are used and informing them that by continuing to use the site, they accept that cookies will be used.

You can choose from different colour schemes for the notice, and it will use the existing default font stack in your theme.

Compatible with ClassicPress.

For further information and support, please visit [the ZigCookie home page](https://www.zigpress.com/plugins/zigcookie/).  Support will ONLY be provided in response to comments posted on that page.

== Installation ==

1. Go to Admin > Plugins > Add New and enter ZigCookie in the search box.
2. Follow the prompts to install and activate the plugin.
